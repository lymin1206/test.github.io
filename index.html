Introduction

Hi all, I am Lie Meng. I obtained my PhD degree from Engineering faculty of University Malaysia Sarawak (UNIMAS) in 2018. Previously, I received my bachelor degree from UNIMAS in 2012. Currently, I am a research associate at Southern Unviersity of Science and Technology (SUSTECH) with Professor Hisao Ishibuchi.

Research interests

Fuzzy systems, soft computing, pattern recognition, evolutionary computation.

Professional membership

- Member, IEEE Computational Intelligence Society
